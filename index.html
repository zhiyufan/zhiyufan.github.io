Bio

I am a PhD student in the School of Computing at the National University of Singapore, advised by Prof. Abhik Roychoudhury and Prof. Shin Hwei Tan. I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the Southern University of Science and Technology in China, where I was advised by Prof. Shin Hwei Tan.

My research interests lie in the areas of automated program repair, large language models, with a focus on intelligent tutoring for programming education.
